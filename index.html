About me:
I am a Professor at Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University.
I received my Ph.D. in 2017 from Harvard University, under the supervision of Prof. Mark Kisin.
I was a Ritt Assistant Professor at Columbia University from 2017 to 2020, and Assistant Professor at University of Maryland from 2020 to 2023.

Research Interest

Number theory and automorphic representations. More specifically, I am interested in the interaction of the following topics as predicted in the Langlands Program:

  • Arithmetic of Shimura varieties, Rapoport-Zink spaces, affine Deligne-Lusztig varieties, etc.
  • Automorphic representations, Arthur-Selberg trace formulas and endoscopy
  • Representation theory of real and p-adic groups.
